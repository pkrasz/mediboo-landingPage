# MediBoo Privacy Policy

## 1. Controller

The controller of the data processed through MediBoo is the team responsible for the MediBoo application.

## 2. What data we process

We may process data provided by the user, such as an email address, care notes, information about app usage, and content submitted through forms.

## 3. Purpose of processing

The data is used to operate the account, maintain the app's features, communicate with the user, and improve and protect the service.

## 4. Legal basis and retention

We store data for as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, or until a valid request for deletion is submitted.

## 5. User rights

1. You have the right to access your data.
2. You have the right to correct it, restrict processing, and request deletion.
3. You can submit a request through the data deletion page.

## 6. Contact

For privacy and data protection matters, use the contact form or the data deletion form available in the service.
